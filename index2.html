Competitive

Competitive Call of Duty is one of the largest E-sports in the world. The largest brands in sports have started to sponsor and support teams in the call of duty community. For example Red Bull, one of the biggest sponsors for extreme sports such as professional downhill mountainbiking, motocross, professional skiing, and many more. has started to sponsor these teams to compete head to head.

What is competitive call of duty?

Well like any other large competitive events, they are sponsored by large companies such as the Call of Duty company, or some others like MLG or some other smaller ones. but they ussually work how any sport would, they do tryouts to see who is worth playing in the event. then, it acts like a ladder system till the point of where 2 teams face off to battle it out for a very large prize. in the 2015 call of duty championships the winners won 1 million dollars.
