Trickshotting

Trickshotting is not a very common part of the call of duty community, there is nothing very competitive about it. But there are still many people who enjoy watching, participating, and making the community of it bigger. But what is trickshotting in Call of Duty?

What is trickshotting?

Trickshotting is in call of duty someone jumps off of an object, spins, and shoots their sniper rifle or throws a throwing knife to kill someone. It is a very much luck based talent, but can help if you are patient and can time things properly. Here is a video showing what it is.
